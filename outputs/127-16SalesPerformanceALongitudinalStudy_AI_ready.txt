Participants and Procedures 
Incumbent Sample.  A sample of 124 incumbent sales 
people (52% male) from a national office product retailer 
was used in this present study.  The target position is a 
business to business consultative sales role.  All 
individuals completed a sales assessment test battery in 
December of 2007 as part of a concurrent validation 
research study examining the assessment validity. The 
ethnic distribution was 82% White/Caucasian, 7% 
Black/African American, 6% Hispanic, 4% Asian. The 
organizational tenure ranged from 14 months to 29 years 
(M=8.29 years, SD=6.18 years).

testing (years 2008, 2009, 2010, and 2011). As expected,
